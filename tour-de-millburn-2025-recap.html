2025 Tour de Millburn • Event Recap

Clear Comms, Smooth Ops, Great Teamwork

Sunday, Sept 28, 2025 • Millburn, NJ

Highlights

  • Weather was beautiful—sunny and warm.
  • Event started on time.
  • Hundreds of cyclists enjoyed the 3 courses (2-mile, 7-mile, 15-mile).
  • Radio communication between ARES volunteers was clear and effective.
  • No major safety incidents along the courses.
  • Event sponsor and the Millburn Police Department were pleased with our performance and efforts.

Thank You

Huge thanks to all ARES volunteers, the event organizers, and the Millburn Police Department. Your teamwork kept the airwaves clean and the riders safe.

We appreciate everyone’s time and professionalism—hope to see you at future events!
